ABOUT PERFECT MOULDS INVESTMENTS

OUR JOURNEY

Building Foundations, Empowering Communities

Established in 2007 Bobonong village, Perfect Moulds Investments has grown into a trusted supplier of high quality construction and building materials. We specialize in providing essential products such as pillars, various types of brick, lintels, paving slabs, and more, catering for both residential and commercial projects.

Our Growth Timeline

  • 2007
    Humble Beginnings

    Founded in Bobonong village with just 3 employees and a small workshop.

  • 2010
    First Expansion

    Added paving slabs to our product line and doubled our workforce.

  • 2015
    Quality Certification

    Received Botswana Bureau of Standards certification for our pillars.

  • 2020
    Modern Facility

    Moved to a new 2,000 sqm production facility in Bobonong.

  • 2024
    Community Impact

    Now employing 50+ local staff and supplying 100+ contractors.

OUR MISSION

To deliver durable, affordable, and reliable building solutions while supporting the growth of Bobonong and its surrounding villages. We take pride in contributing to local development by supplying materials that lay the foundation for stronger, safer and more sustainable structures.

WHY CHOOSE US?

  • Wide range of products - From pillars to paving, we offer everything needed for construction and landscape.
  • Local expertise - Over 17 years of serving Bobonong and neighboring communities.
  • Quality and Trust - Committed to supplying materials that meet industry standards.
  • Community focus - Proudly supporting local builders, contractors and home owners.
